Manage video media and integrate playlists from your AnyClip account into your WordPress-powered site

== Description ==

Search and manage video content from your AnyClip account and seamlessly integrate players, clips and playlists into your WordPress-powered site with the AnyClip WordPress Plugin. 
 
Drawing upon the full power of AnyClip’s Luminous Video Platform, AnyClip’s WordPress plugin integrates with studio workflows, using AI content data to empower easy content search, management, measurement and distribution.

Features include:
- Embed videos directly to article pages quickly and easily
- Create video playlists and embed to article pages quickly and easily
- View historic content performance data at the time of clip selection to spotlight videos known to be most interesting to your audience
- View recommendations for relevant, top-performing or most-recent videos to build more engaging playlists
- Track clip performance by engagement, revenue performance and other metrics
- AI-Powered content search allows for instant access and easy navigation of the to the entirety of your AnyClip owned and licensed content library

== Installation ==

1. Pre-requisite: this plugin requires having an active AnyClip publishers' account. To apply for an account, please contact AnyClip: https://anyclip.com/contact-us/
2. Upload the plugin files to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory, or install the plugin through the WordPress plugins screen directly.
3. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' screen in WordPress
4. Create a new post or page, add a title, save the post/page, add AnyClip block (Media), login to your AnyClip account
